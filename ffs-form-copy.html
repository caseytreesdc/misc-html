Casey Trees has programs available for Washington DC homeowners to plant on their property. Before moving forward with this form, be sure that one of these low-cost programs doesn't already fit your needs:

  • TREE REBATE -

    Plant a tree at home and we will pay you back! You pick the tree you want, plant it and receive up to $100 back per tree. Click here for more information.

  • FREE TREES DC - Get Free Trees to accentuate your home with a beautiful shade tree at no cost. To register, submit an free trees application and one of our professional Urban Foresters 

Casey Trees has programs that enable communities, neighborhoods, and schools to plant in public and private spaces. Before moving forward with this form, be sure that one of these community planting programs does not already meet your needs:

  • SCHOOL TREE PLANTING - Organize a learning opportunity for students! We re-tree school campuses with the help of students, faculty, and parents. Click here to see more information and apply.
  • COMMUNITY TREE PLANTING - Organize a community or neighborhood tree planting! Approved applicants receive trees, tools, technical assistance, and help recruiting volunteers. here to see more information and apply.

Casey Trees does not service street trees.

Street trees - which are usually located in the public space between the street and the sidewalk - are planted and maintained by the District's Urban Forestry Administration (UFA), a department of DDOT.

Requests for a street tree to be inspected, planted, pruned or removed can be made by calling the service request center at 311 or making the request online. Hold on to your confirmation number to track progress on your request. 

Casey Trees only performs pruning services on young trees.

We encourage homeowners to prune and care for their trees themselves, whenever possible. For resources on how to prune, plant, and care for trees, please visit our trees "how-to" webpage.

Casey Trees does not usually perform tree removal services.

Casey Trees may perform these services on trees that have been planted by Casey Trees, if they are small, or within the scope of replacing the removed tree by planting another. but otherwise refer you to our partners at Bartlett Tree Experts
