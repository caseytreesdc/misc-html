Countdown to Arbor Day April 22nd, 2023

We challenge YOU to take the Earth Day Challenge

Join Casey Trees’ inaugural youth Earth Day Challenge! Schools are invited to showcase trees on their campus and how their school can make a difference to the urban forest. Casey Trees will highlight the best in each category in our Leaflet newsletter. Scroll below to find instructions for each mini challenge. Participating schools are entered into consideration for a Junior Urban Forester Field Trip with our Education staff! Other prizes include Casey Trees swag, a future tree planting, and more. Each school will be considered for one prize, and winners will be announced in early May.

Earth Day Challenges

Participate in one or more of our challenges. Only one submission from each school will be awarded.
  • Challenge 1: Poster contest
    • Craft a 18″ x 24″ poster with one climate solution your school will implement. Winning designs will be featured in our Leaflet newsletter with your school’s giving link.
  • Challenge 2: Video PSA or Earth Day Poem or rap read by student(s).
    • Go viral! Submit a one-minute video about how your school goes green, or how you are a friend to trees in your neighborhood.
    • Submit a one-minute video of an Earth Day rap or read poem by your students.
    • Please upload your video PSA or read poem/rap to YouTube, Dropbox or Google and submit a link to the submission form.
    • Poems can also be submitted in art form as a photo.
  • Challenge 3: Spring Break week Leaf and Ecosystem Exploration
    • Use Casey Trees leaf science worksheets to discover qualities about leaves in your neighborhood:
      • Leaf Exploration worksheet
      • Ecosystem Inventory worksheet
    • BONUS: Discover more about leaves using these worksheets:
      • Simple vs Compound Leaves
      • Opposite vs Alternate Leaves
    • Early learners (aged 3-5) can complete these tree exploration activities:
      • Tree Bingo
      • Tree Design activity
    • Winners in this category will be considered for a Casey Trees swag item
  • Challenge 4: Tree Care Relay
    • Pledge to care for trees at your school. Send us 3 pictures of a tree care event, where you water and clean up around your trees.
